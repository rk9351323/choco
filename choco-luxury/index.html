Our Heritage

A Century of Passion

Founded in 1920 by Master Chocolatier Francois Dubois in a small atelier in Paris, CHOCO. was born out of a profound passion for the cacao bean. What began as a local obsession quickly became a global synonym for luxury and craftsmanship.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients nature has to offer, and meticulously craft them into edible masterpieces. We believe that chocolate is more than a treat—it is an experience, a moment of pure bliss that transcends the ordinary.

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers in Venezuela, Ecuador, and Madagascar to ensure fair compensation and sustainable farming practices.

Premium Ingredients

Only the top 1% of the world's cacao beans are selected for our chocolate, combined with pure cocoa butter and real Madagascar vanilla.

Master Craftsmanship

Each piece is handcrafted by artisans who have spent decades perfecting the intricate art of tempering, molding, and finishing.
